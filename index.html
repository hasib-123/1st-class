T20 World Cup 2022 Schedule

T20 World Cup 2022 Schedule: The ICC T20 World Cup 2022 is scheduled to take place
between October 16, 2022 – November 13, 2022. The tournament consists of 45 matches
in total planned to host across venues – Adelaide, Brisbane, Geelong, Hobart,
Melbourne, Perth, and Sydney.

The final will be hosted under lights at the MCG on 13 November 2022. The semi-finals
will be hosted at the Sydney Cricket Ground and Adelaide Oval on 9 and 10 November,
respectively.

Australia(Host), Afghanistan, Bangladesh, England, India, Pakistan, New Zealand, and
South Africa were confirmed in the Super 12s, whereas Namibia, Scotland, Sri Lanka,
and West Indies will be playing the qualifiers preceding the main draw.
